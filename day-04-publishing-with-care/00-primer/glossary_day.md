# Day 04 Glossary

- **Publishing with care**: practices that keep data safe, explainable, and respectful.
- **Publish package**: bundled data plus documentation and checks ready for reuse.
- **DOI**: persistent identifier that makes citation and version clarity possible.
- **Versioning**: tagging releases so changes are traceable.
- **LICENSE**: document that states reuse permissions and restrictions.
- **CITATION.cff**: machine- and human-readable citation file for your package.
- **Zenodo metadata**: JSON (a structured text format for storing data) that describes your deposit and powers the DOI landing page.
- **Practice path**: preparing everything locally without depositing.
- **Live path**: depositing to Zenodo and minting a DOI.
